Afro Samurai

This is a short but well written story about a samurai with no fear for blood and no mercy for murder. His dad killed, he is alone in the world, and has developed a thirst for vengeance among the years. Seeking for the ultimate battle against the owner of the No. 1 headband, he kills everyone that stands in his path.
